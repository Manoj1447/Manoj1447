NATIONAL CURRICULUM FRAMEWORK FOR SCHOOL EDUCATION 2023

The National Curriculum Framework for School EducationThe – Pre-Draft is uploaded for consultation. While giving your
feedback,it need to be kept in view that this is a pre-draft of the NCF-SE which is still in the process of making. Rounds of discussion
within the National Steering Committee are also going on. Feedback from diverse stakeholders will further help NSC to look critically
into different modalities and approaches, this framework is proposing.
